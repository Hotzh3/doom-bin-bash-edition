# World 2 Polish Notes

## Goal

Make World 2 feel like a portfolio showcase without changing its gameplay spine.

The route should read as:

- cold ion descent
- sulfur lattice pressure
- nadir-glow release
- Bloom Warden pit as the final authored statement

## What Changed

- Reveal corridors now lead into clearer exit spaces.
- Recovery windows are being used as fake calm, not dead air.
- Boss arena lighting is layered into rim, core, bloom bed, and exit reads.
- Bloom Warden has less of an HP-sponge feel and more of a staged duel identity.

## Playtest Checklist

- The fracture route should feel like a descent, not a flat hallway.
- The threshold coda should briefly quiet down before the final move.
- Sulfur lattice should still feel hostile, but with enough light hierarchy to parse routes.
- The Bloom Warden arena should read in three layers: safe-ish rim for movement, bright core for threat focus, bloom bed for visual tension.
- No beat should obscure the player silhouette for long enough to break combat readability.
- If the boss still feels too tanky, lower `maxHealth` in `RaycastWorldTwoLevels.ts` before touching behavior.
